In Case of Zombie Attack

Hopefully you have taken precautions against this inevitability (see Prevent Your Death). At this point there are several things you can do.

  • Do not panic
  • Remain in your group
  • Choose the most appropriate formation
  • If using guns fire at the head
  • Be ready to commit to a strategic retreat

This will be much more difficult if you have not heeded the precautions necessary, such as no armor or not remaining in a group. At this point your best option is to run and hide.
