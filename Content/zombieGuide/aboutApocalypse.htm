About the Apocalypse

The apocalypse was a global event that occurred more than 100 years ago.

It began in New York city at a research facility and spread across the world in a month.

Most facilities are now abandoned and infrastructure is no longer maintained.

Global population has fallen to dangerously low levels. It is unclear whether the race will survive.
