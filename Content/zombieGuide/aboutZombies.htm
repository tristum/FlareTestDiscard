About Zombies

Zombies are undead creatures. They were once alive but now are walking corpses. The CDC, before it was completely destroyed by a walking time bomb provided the following definition:

"A zombie is an animated body of a previously living creature. It does not feel pain and has a one minded focus to consume temporal fluid. A zombie can be any once living creature."

Some key factors to know about zombies

  • They can only be "killed" by cutting off their heads or burning them
  • Their bite will turn you into a zombie
  • They hunt in packs

Zombie spores

If a dispatched zombie is not burned or buried they will begin to give off spores after an hour or so. At this point, if the spores are breathed in there is a good chance that you will become a zombie in under a day.
